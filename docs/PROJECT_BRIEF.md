# iThoddoo Maldives Project Brief

iThoddoo Maldives is a premium travel platform for Thoddoo Island, Maldives.

The first goal is to sell excursions and transfers.

Future goals:
- AI Concierge
- Guesthouse listings
- Accommodation marketplace
- Excursion booking system
- Transfer schedules
- Multilingual support
- Interactive island map
- Online payments
- Business dashboard
- Expansion to nearby islands

Brand feeling:
Modern, premium, trustworthy, local, tropical, luxury, mobile-first.

Main guest actions:
- Book excursions
- Check transfer options
- Contact by WhatsApp
- Explore Thoddoo travel guide
- Discover beaches, restaurants, shops, and activities
